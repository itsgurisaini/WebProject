About Us

As you approach Flying Moose Heli Skiing Chalet, breathtaking views of the Selkirk and Monashee Mountains and the Columbia River Valley are the backdrop for exquisite living spaces that include eight luxury bedroom suites. The Chalet is the ultimate Canadian four-season vacation destination for skiers, hikers, cyclists, golfers, aquatic adventurers and bridal parties looking to make memories in a truly remarkable setting. With available services from a Butler and a Private Chef to daily Housekeeping, discerning guests enjoy incredible pampering, gourmet meals and an unforgettable stay with complete discretion and privacy.
